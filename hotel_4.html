Hôtel Le Méridien N'Fis

Relax in stylish splendor at Le Méridien N'Fis. Located in the Menara District of Marrakech, Morocco, our resort hotel is nestled amidst lush parkland, just minutes from the vibrant city center. Find a stylish respite in chic accommodations with plush bedding, high-speed Wi-Fi and signature amenities. Step onto your scenic terrace and gaze out at the illuminated Andalusian gardens outside our hotel. Delight your palate with locally inspired fare and international favorites at our distinctive hotel restaurants. Celebrate and succeed in flexible event space with modern AV equipment, expert planners and custom catering options. Explore celebrated local attractions including Jamaa el-Fna Square and Menara Mall, along with great shopping, dining
